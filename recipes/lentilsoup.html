Lentil Soup

Description

Vegetarian comfort food that is perfect for a cold day, this lentil soup recipe is a perfect recipe for nutritious comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cups dry lentils
  • 8 cups water
  • 1(14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup spinach, rinsed and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
